About

Myself

As a person who did not have an academic background in computer science, I decided to apply to the graduate certificate program in computer science to learn and gain the skills and knowledge in the areas of programming. After 2 years I have earned a graduate certificate of computer science foundations at Kennesaw University. I have a strong passion for learning and creating responsive website applications and I am a motivative web developer who is looking to apply extensive skills to software development field.

I enjoy building responsive interfaces with HTML, CSS and JavaScript using the lastest UI frameworks and techniques. I know that being a software developer will never be"just a job" for me. It offers an engaging challenge to continually learn and improve my technical skills every single day.
